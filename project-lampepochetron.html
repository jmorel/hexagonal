Lampe de
poche-tron

Bad puns, beautiful bottles.
Let there be light!

Well you see, in french, a lampe de poche is a flashlight and a pochetron is a drunkard. Since the bottle acts as a flashlight (batteries are embedded within the cap) and since I had to drink the bottle to put the lights in… Lampe de poche, pochetron, lampe de poche-tron.

I knew explaining a pun was a bad idea.
